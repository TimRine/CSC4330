FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Questions

Q: What type of events can I use PlanIt for?

A: PlanIt can be used for any sort of event of any size! From a small birthday party to a corporate trip across the country, the tools PlanIt offers are for you to decide how to use. If you think you need a little more organization to your event, then PlanIt can give you all the tools that you want, such as using our calendar to set a date for a hotel reservation deadline or using our broadcast system to tell your entire group of a change in location.

Q: Can I use PlanIt on my smartphone?

A: Yes! Our application works on mobile versions of browsers!

Q: On the "About Us" page on PlanIt, are those titles for your team members the actual titles of your job?

A: Of course! Without titles like those, how would we ever organize ourselves to help you organize your events? Our leaders pride themselves on their titles and responsibilities!
