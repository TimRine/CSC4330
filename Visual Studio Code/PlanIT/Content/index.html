What is PlanIt?

Well, I'm glad you asked! PlanIt is a web application designed to help you organize your events or trips! With tools like a group calendar, broadcasts and chat, and polls, you can better manage a small event, like a party, or even a huge event like a family vacation across the country. Our goal is to give our customers a detailed and customizable way to organize your events and groups! Click the button on the right to start creating your event!

Create an Event!

Set Dates With PlanIt's Calendar

PlanIt's built-in calendar gives you the ability to tell all your invited guests when important dates are, like reservation deadlines or even the big day of your event!

Broadcast Information To Your Group!

Need to tell everyone in your group something important, but a simple group message loses information too quickly? Well, PlanIt let's you broadcast information to all of your invited guests so your guests will never be confused.

Create Polls To Ask Your Guests What They Want!

PlanIt lets you create polls for your guests to let them tell you what they would like! Now you'll never have to worry about guessing about what your guests would prefer!
