Broadcast Information and Chat

If you have important information you need everyone to hear, our broadcast features lets you tell everyone what they need to know for your event

Sometimes, plans change and you need to inform your group of these changes. Or sometimes, you just need to tell everyone something important. PlanIt gives you the ability to broadcast information so you can minimize confusion. As opposed to regular chat services where information can easily get pushed up as more people converse, PlanIt gives you the solution to avoiding that problem!
